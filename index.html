The Detergent Crisis

Conventional detergents significantly pollute our water systems due to high levels of phosphate compounds. These compounds trigger an unnatural explosion in phytoplankton populations (severe algae blooms).

This excessive algae blocks sunlight and heavily depletes the dissolved oxygen supply in rivers, disrupting the respiratory processes of local fish species, causing mass mortality and threatening overall aquatic biodiversity.

Nature's Cleaning Power

Alcazyme is a revolutionary, 100% biodegradable liquid detergent. It utilizes natural plant saponins from Aloe vera for cleansing and fermented organic waste (Eco Enzyme) to tackle stubborn stains while actively preserving our vital aquatic ecosystems.

The addition of locally-sourced Cananga flowers introduces a premium natural aroma, solving the typical "unpleasant smell" issue of natural detergents without using synthetic chemicals.

Keajaiban Fermentasi Organik

Apa itu Eco Enzyme?

Eco Enzyme adalah cairan multifungsi yang dihasilkan dari proses fermentasi limbah organik dapur (seperti kulit buah dan sayuran), gula merah muda (molase), dan air. Cairan ini merupakan kunci utama dari daya bersih Alcazyme.

  • Proses Pembuatan (Rasio 1:3:10)

    Difermentasi secara anaerob selama 3 bulan tanpa sinar matahari. Menggunakan rasio emas: 1 bagian gula merah, 3 bagian kulit buah (jeruk, nanas, semangka, melon, buah naga), dan 10 bagian air bersih.

  • Fungsi & Kandungan Aktif

    Sangat kaya akan enzim amilase, protease, dan lipase yang secara aktif memecah noda organik membandel (lemak, protein, pati). Berfungsi ganda sebagai sabun cair, disinfektan, hingga penjernih air sungai yang tercemar.

  • Keuntungan Maksimal

    Sangat ekonomis, terbuat dari bahan alami yang aman, dan secara langsung membantu mengurangi volume limbah organik rumah tangga yang berakhir di tempat pembuangan akhir.

Natural Alchemy

4 Powerful Core Ingredients

Meticulously selected and blended in a 150:25:1 ratio for optimal performance.

Aloe Vera (Aloe vera)

Rich in natural saponins acting as natural surfactants to produce lather and bind dirt particles.

Eco Enzyme

Produced by anaerobic fermentation of fruit peels. Contains enzymes that actively break down stains.

Cananga Flowers (Cananga odorata)

Locally cultivated in Banjarmasin, these flowers replace synthetic fragrances with a rich natural scent.

Baking Soda (Sodium bicarbonate)

With an alkaline pH and highly abrasive properties, it aggressively lifts stubborn organic stains.

Proses Pengolahan

How Alcazyme is Made

1

Eco Enzyme Fermentation

We utilize discarded fruit peels (orange, pineapple, watermelon, dragon fruit) sourced locally. A 3-month anaerobic fermentation process is used with a strict ratio of 1 part brown sugar : 3 parts fruit peels : 10 parts clean water.

2

Extraction

100g of Aloe vera is peeled and mashed to extract the core gel. 100g of Cananga flowers are boiled for 3 minutes to produce a fragrant natural extract.

3

Formulation

The cooled Cananga extract is blended with 100g of Aloe vera gel, 100ml of aged Eco Enzyme, and 4g of baking soda until a homogenous, aromatic detergent is formed.
